Dev // Design

As a true product of my environment growing up in la Mission, in San Francisco, the many murals that coat streets have always inspired me. Always obsessed over the historical context, line-weights, colors, and over all design of the murals, I started drawing at a very early age. During the doc-com boom, the Mission district started to change due to the technology and entrepreneur influence from Silicon Valley, which influenced my interest in technology and entrepreneurship.
